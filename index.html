John Wick former assassin retires to take care of his terminal ill wife. He receives one final gift from his wife to find meaning in his life and pace after his beloved wife is gone, the gift is a dog who his wife named Daisy. Russian mob prince Losef Tarasov wanted to buy John Wick 1969 Mustang and Wick refused the offer. Losef is used to getting what he wants so him and his men broke into Wick’s house to steal 1969 Mustang and his wife’s present. Blind with revenge of destruction against the sophisticated kingpin, Viggo Tarasov and his family who are fully aware of Wick lethal capacity. Viggo Tarasov refers Wick as the “bogeyman”.

Author: Francesca Walters (P2546257)
